Astronomy in Learning

It has been proven in studies that students who engage in astronomy during primary or secondary school are more likely to pursue careers, professions, or jobs in science and technology, and to keep up to date with scientific discoveries. This does not just benefit the field of astronomy, but reaches across other scientific disciplines as well. Source



Astronomy in Uniting

Astronomy is a collaborative effort. During 1887, astronomers from around the world put together their telescope images to create the first map of the entire sky. Today, astronomers travel around the globe to attend conferences, and learn from one another. Source
